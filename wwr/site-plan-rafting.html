Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to give easier access to information about the rafting company to potential customers.

Audience

Who are the target customers?

-- The target customers are travelers or nearby residents who are looking for an ourdoor adventure activity!

What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website?

-- This website will give information about what they can expect during the activity, prices, locations, reviews, etc.

How will my audience usually access my site?

-- This website will be accessed by desktops, laptops, and mobile devices.
